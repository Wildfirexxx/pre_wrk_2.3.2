Bio

Click image for full bio

Foolio was born Charles Jones on June 21,1998. He is an up and coming Jacksonville Florida artist. Foolio currently resides in Atlanta Georgia due to rival gang beefs that have claimed the lives of many of his friends.

The artist began to buzz nationally after his hit remix of Fantasia's "When I see you" dropped in 2021. Using that momentum Foolio collaberated with Zaytoven to create the mix tape 6toven.
